# **App Name**: Affectionately Yours

## Core Features:

- Heartfelt Message Display: Display a sincere apology and expression of love from Nikhil to Urvashe.
- Cute Bar Customization: A special element highlighting '8th September' (the day Nikhil started liking Urvashe), customizable in appearance.
- Personal Photo Integration: Incorporate 3 personal photos into the card design. One of the picture show both Urvashe and Nikhil, another shows fan and roof and one of the last one is with only Nikhl smiling to show Nikhl's expression of sincerity to Urvashe
- Dynamic animation and rendering tool: Employ a subtle animation for a heartwarming effect using AI to add in special personalized rendering.
- Signature attribution: Display clearly that the card has been written by Nikhil to emphasize sincerity
- Sincerity prompt for AI enhancement: Prompt to generative AI tool to give creative design direction that should match sincerity to express depth seriousness of apology.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Soft rose (#F4DCD4) to evoke feelings of love and tenderness.
- Background color: Light cream (#F7F5EB) for a gentle and warm backdrop.
- Accent color: Dusty rose (#D1A6A3), a slightly deeper hue, to bring a gentle contrast to CTAs and key interactive elements, such as the 'cute bar'.
- Body and headline font: 'Alegreya', a humanist serif, which expresses elegance and a contemporary feel for both the heartfelt message and additional text.
- Implement subtle, heartwarming animations such as a gentle fade-in or a soft pulsing effect to enhance emotional connection.
- Emphasize the heartfelt message prominently to express the sender's feelings clearly and sincerely. A custom 'cute bar' and heartwarming animations will serve as delicate emotional reinforcements.
